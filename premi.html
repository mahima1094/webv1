About the Workshop

The "Ophthalmic AI" workshop aims to bring together researchers, clinicians, and industry experts to discuss the latest advancements in AI applications for eye health. From Oculomics to Generative AI for retinal imaging, we explore how machine intelligence can solve critical challenges in Blindness prevention.
